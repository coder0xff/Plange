This project began in 2013. My experience in the software development ecosystem the two decades prior led me to this conclusion: there is much left to be desired in the power and flexibility of modern programming languages and tools.



Plange began as a bulleted list of features and ideas. Existing languages were used as inspiration for this list - their successes and also their failings. This approach has lead to a language with a very large scope. Though ambitious, I hope to make a meaningful contribution to the comparatively infantile domains of computer science and software engineering.



The name is a reference to the 4th episode of the series "IT Crowd". See the clip here .
